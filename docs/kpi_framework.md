# KPI Framework

## Executive Narrative KPI

### Revenue-to-Profit Rank Divergence Score
Measures the gap between SKU revenue ranking and actual profit contribution ranking.

Purpose:
Identify products that appear commercially successful while contributing weak profitability performance.

---

## Core Profitability KPIs

### Net Profit Ratio per SKU
Measures retained profitability relative to revenue generation at SKU level.

Business Risk:
High revenue can mask weak or negative retained profit performance.

### Negative Profit Order Concentration
Measures the proportion of transactions generating negative profit contribution.

Business Risk:
Average profitability may conceal concentrated transactional losses.

---

## Operational Efficiency KPIs

### Late Delivery Risk Concentration Index
Measures whether operational delivery risk is disproportionately concentrated among specific products relative to order share.

Business Risk:
Operational inefficiency may scale alongside revenue concentration.

### Discount Dependency Rate
Measures the proportion of transactions requiring discounting support to sustain sales volume.

Business Risk:
Commercial growth may rely on structurally weak pricing performance.

---

## Stability & Risk KPIs

### Profit Ratio Volatility
Measures the consistency of SKU profitability performance across transactions.

Business Risk:
High profitability instability reduces forecasting reliability and operational predictability.

---

## Interpretation Standard

No KPI is treated as conclusive in isolation.

Operational risk findings require multiple weak signals to converge consistently at meaningful transaction volume.
