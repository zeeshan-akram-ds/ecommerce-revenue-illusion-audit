# Project Scope

## Primary Objective
This project investigates how revenue-focused reporting can hide operational and profitability problems inside an e-commerce business.

The audit focuses on identifying:
- Revenue-to-profitability distortion
- Inventory cash traps
- Operational drag from shipping, lead times, and defect rates
- Stockout and inventory aging risk
- Financial exposure under operational stress scenarios

## Included Areas
- Profitability analysis
- Inventory and operational risk analysis
- Statistical validation
- Scenario simulation
- Decision-focused reporting

## Explicit Exclusions
This project does not cover:
- Customer segmentation
- Marketing attribution
- Recommendation systems
- NLP or review analysis
- Dynamic pricing systems
- Deep learning applications
- Causal inference claims

## Intended Audience
- E-commerce founders
- Operators
- Business analysts
- BI professionals
- Operational decision-makers

## Dataset Constraints

This project uses transactional and operational order data rather than direct warehouse or ERP inventory data.

As a result:
- Inventory-related metrics are modeled estimates
- Supplier-specific conclusions are avoided
- Operational risk analysis focuses primarily on fulfillment efficiency, discount dependency, and profitability distortion
